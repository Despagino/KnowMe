Greatest Lesson

I realized at a young age that life is fragile. When I was 13, my dad (a missionary) was assigned in Tacloban, Leyte, Philippines. In this time, the strongest Typhoon ever recorded, Typhoon " Haiyan" had just devoured Tacloban and my family and I were in the heart of the aftermath. Many people lost their loved ones. I'm talking about hundreds and thousands of lives were lost. This was the greatest lesson I've ever received, that life will always be needed to be treasured and appreciated.
